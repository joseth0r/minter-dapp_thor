Boujee Leopards Club

Boujee Leopards Club is an exclusive NFT project with 1,333 NFTs. Created with a purpose to offer holders the opportunity to participate in the next frontier of the digital world. Cryptocurrency is growing at exponential rates, NFTs in specific are a new realm opportunity, full of new utilities, exclusive communities, and provide real world as well as virtual value. Additionally, certain NFT projects such as the Boujee Leopards Club offer a gateway into the Metaverse, where members can explore, monetize, earn passive income and create digital realities of their own. You will have the opportunity to own one of 6,590 Boujee Leopard NFTs.

Why 6,590? Sadly, there are only 6,590 beautiful and majestic Snow Leopards left on Planet Earth. Boujee Leopards Club is not just about authentic art, but also about unmatched real-world and Metaverse utilities. We are a community dedicated to making a real difference!

Story

During a beautiful frosty evening, there were 4 Snow Leopards, who were as ‘Boujee’ as they come. They all sat surrounding a bonfire somewhere in the jungle with some chilled intergalactic beer and psychedelic joints. One of the leopards noticed a wooden box in the bushes.

Deciding to take a closer look, the group got up and went towards it, the box had the initials ‘MVPG’ carved on it. Finding this box took them off guard because of the value it seemed to hold and being found abandoned in the forest and in seemingly perfect condition. One of the Leopards, who was the nervous and paranoid one in the group, advised against opening this unknown and strange box. Reminding them about many movies, books, and stories where situations like this rarely go well.

But before he could even complete his words, the adventurous and risk-taking Leopard, possibly fueled by his joint or beer, dismissed his friend and opened the box, and ZAP, an intense beam of light came out of the box straight into the sky. The light was blinding and within an instant, the light was gone and so were the Leopards. When they opened their eyes, they looked around and realized that they were in a place that they had never seen before, someplace futuristic to say the least. While they were exploring this new place, a civilization known as the Meta Hunters: the keepers of the Metaverse, sense that some unidentified creatures had found their way into their home.

Hunting to find these unidentified creatures and send them back to where they came from. Utilizing their futuristic equipment, and tracking the trail left by the leopards, the Meta Hunters tracked these Snow Leopards in no time. The Leopards were surrounded by several Meta Hunter drones. All hope seemed to be lost.

Using quick instincts, and noticing an item that wasn’t in his pocket before, the leader amongst the four leopards took out a pen-like device and pressed the button on top of it. A beautiful gate of light opened in front of them, shooting a blinding light in every direction, as the light faded the portal became clear. The Meta Hunters could see thousands of Boujee Leopards charging, ready to enter the Metaverse. Why are they here? What’s gonna happen next? Is this the start of a war? Or is it the start of something even crazier? No one knows yet, but we’re all going to find out very soon!

Why Snow Leopards ?

Found in the mountainous regions of Central and Southern Asia, these ghosts of the mountain are some of the most beautiful and majestic creatures that roam this planet. However, these beautiful creatures are hard to find, due to excessive hunting of this species, there are only 6,590 of them left on Earth. Through the Boujee Leopard Club, we want to raise awareness about these Snow Leopards and how imminent the danger is to the species. We want to do whatever we can to help prevent their extinction. We have planned to donate $100,000 USD to a wildlife foundation for Snow Leopards. The Boujee Leopards Club wants to give the Snow Leopards a second home in the Metaverse.
